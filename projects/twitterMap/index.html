Racial Activism On Twitter

by Linh Huynh & Leah Welch

On March 16, 2021, a series of mass shootings occurred at three spas or massage parlors in the metropolitan area of Atlanta, Georgia, United States. Eight people were killed, six of whom were Asian women, and one other person was wounded.

This tragic events brought the wave of anti-Asian racism into the national dialogue about racial activism.

The Twitter community has responded to the rise of racist acts through an outpouring of activism, highlighting the ties between racism toward Asian communities and former President Trump's classification of COVID-19 as "the China Virus".
